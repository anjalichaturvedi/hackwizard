Hack Wizard: A 48-hours virtual hackathon

⚡️ABOUT

WHO ARE WE?

Superposition New Delhi with a team of 9 amazing women and 1 mission-to bridge the gender gap in STEM is organizing Hack Wizard

WHAT IS HACK WIZARD?

Hack Wizard is a 48 hours virtual hackathon for women. Participants will form teams, code projects, attend workshops/panels, network and most of all, have a lot of fun!

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

Any women (female and nonbinary) in high school or college are more than welcome to attend the hackathon. No coding experience is required.
